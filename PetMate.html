PetMate

Pet parents face challenges in finding trustworthy pet sitters, often lacking a reliable way to verify the credibility and experience of potential sitters. Our website aims to address this issue by creating a LinkedIn-like platform where pet parents can easily discover and connect with sitters who have previously worked within their social circles, enhancing trust through familiar connections.
